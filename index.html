Who We Are

About

Founded in 2006, Lords Recruiter Ministry exists to proclaim Jesus, make disciples, and serve our city. Our name reflects our mission: to be anchored in Christ and to recruit souls into the Kingdom of God.

  • Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • The Church is Christ’s body, called to make disciples.
  • Jesus will return; we live in hope and holiness.
